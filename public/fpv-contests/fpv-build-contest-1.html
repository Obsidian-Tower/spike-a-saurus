Contest Rules

  1. Please watch the contest video (YouTube Link). It explains the theme, guidelines, and scoring criteria.
  2. Submit your build by June 9, 2025 at 23:59 UTC. (One week from June 2, 2025; no late submissions accepted.)
  3. Your submission must be in a Minecraft 1.16.5 world format. We will not accept maps saved in any other version. After unzipping, the folder inside your “saves” folder should show “level.dat” and a “region/” subfolder.
